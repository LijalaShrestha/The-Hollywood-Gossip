SELENA GOMEZ: 'I WANNA SHOW PEOPLE THAT I CAN SING'

“I just wanna perform. I’m not trying to dance as much as I did the last run,” the new face of Coca-Cola’s Share-a-Coke campaign tells The US Magazine. “I really — I just wanna sing. And I wanna show people that I can sing.” - Selena Gomez

In an interview with Us Weekly Video, Selena Gomez spoke about wanting to prove her vocals on her upcoming Revival Tour.
“I have never cared about a tour as much as I care about this one. I’ve never worked as hard,” she says.
Selena even says that she has never been more excited since her performance at 'Star Dance' tour concert in 2013.

“Every day I start with a workout, I do vocal lessons, I do piano lessons,” she explains. “I go on stage and we map out the whole thing.”

ADAM LEVINE AND BEHATI PRINSLOO HAVING A BABY GIRL

Adam Levine confirms that he is having a baby girl with his wife, Behati Prinsloo, on the episode of Live with Kelly and Michael.

“I’m about to be a dad. I’m superpumped,” says Levine, 37, to the show hosts, Michael Strehen and Kelly Ripa about his first time fatherhood. Levine even mentioned Prinsloo's, Victoria's Secret Angel, 26, number 1 craving. “She’s good, man, she’s just, you know, been eating a lot of weird food. Watermelon, that’s the thing in our house. A lot — so much watermelon,” he explains. “Maybe she just, like, really likes watermelon,” Levine added. “I mean, it’s delicious. I’m glad it’s not, like, pickles or some weird thing.” The US Magazine exclusively broke the news of Prinsloo’s pregnancy on March 10. A source told at the time that she and Levine were “over the moon” about impending parenthood.
